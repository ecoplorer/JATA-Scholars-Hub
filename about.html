About Jigawa Agricultural Commercialization Workforce Scholarship

Empowering Jigawa State's agricultural transformation through international education

Workforce development, innovation, commercialization
Led by JATA International exposure Jobs and value chains

The Jigawa Agricultural Transformation Agency (JATA) is a strategic government agency established to lead the state's agricultural transformation agenda. Under the leadership of His Excellency, the Governor of Jigawa State, Malam Umar Namadi, FCA, JATA drives innovation, commercialization, and job creation through targeted partnerships and workforce development.

This scholarship initiative is designed to develop world-class human capital in modern agricultural practices, agribusiness, and technology, using international education and exposure to global best practices.

What the scholarship delivers
  • Build a pipeline of skilled professionals for agricultural modernization and commercialization
  • Strengthen research capability, innovation, and technology adoption in agriculture
  • Support climate-smart agriculture, digital agriculture, and value chain development
  • Require graduates to return and contribute to Jigawa's development priorities

Return and impact
Scholarship beneficiaries are expected to return and support priority value chains, research, extension services, and commercialization outcomes in Jigawa State.
